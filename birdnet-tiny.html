We also provide training and deployment tools for microcontrollers. BirdNET-Tiny Forge simplifies the training of BirdNET-Tiny models and their deployment on embedded devices.


BirdNET-Tiny Forge is a collaboration between the BirdNET-team and fold ecosystemics. Start building your own tiny models for bioacoustics here: https://github.com/birdnet-team/BirdNET-Tiny-Forge.

Note

BirdNET-Tiny Forge is under active development, so you might encounter changes that could affect your current workflow. We recommend checking for updates regularly.
